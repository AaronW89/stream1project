Ann Marie Webb was born in Chester England and now lives in Co. Wicklow. Solo exhibitions include the Cavanacor

Gallery, Lifford, Co. Down. Recent group shows include MA Graduate exhibition, HSC Building Thomas Street, Dublin,

Mermaid Open, Bray Co. Wicklow, Stranger Stranger, The complex, Dublin, EU-Topia, Block H Market Studios, Dublin,

(RE)Structure, Eight Gallery Dublin. Beautiful Future, Hive, Waterford. RHA group show, Short listed for Most promising

Graduate, won the Peter O'Kane Award, RDS, and A member of Raft. She has a BA in Visual Arts Practice, IADT

and has just completed her MFA in painting. Ann Marie has also taught a wide range of groups for 15 years. She is a

member of the collective RAFT.
